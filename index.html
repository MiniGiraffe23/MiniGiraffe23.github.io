Resident Evil 7: Biohazard

Resident Evil 7: Biohazard is a survival horror game that has been rumored to make some people wet themselves. The game was developed by Capcom, and realeased in January 2017. This is the eleventh installment in the Resident Evil series. It is the first to be played from a first-person perspective and be compatible with the Playstation VR (Virtual Reality).
