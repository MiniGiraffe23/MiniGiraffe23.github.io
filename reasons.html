My Thoughts on Resident Evil 7: Biohazard

Overall, I really enjoy this new installment of Resident Evil, it might actually be my all time favorite ... I'll get back to you on that.

The game offers a good amount of content, a great story and a good scare for those who enjoy horror. It keeps you interested, challenges you and doesn't fail to offer a few surprises here and there.


Pros & Cons of RE7: Biohazard
Feature Pro Con
Gameplay First person, realisitc and creepy gameplay Controls can get kinda clunky during boss fights making it difficult to maneuver
Content Great story with twists and turns, puzzles to get you thinking, keeps you on your toes and there's hours of gameplay plus DLC to keep you intersted Would have liked a more robus move/ability set for the main character instead of just relying upon the weapons found
Weapon Choices Good amount of weapon choices - machine guns, pistols, flamethrower, etc Would have liked more melee weapons. If I pass a bat I should be able to pick it up, same goes for a hammer or crowbar.
